So we are not cooking a horse, dont worry! this is a delicious meat preparation that originates from colombia, why is it called like that? no idea!

The following list will give you the needed ingredients for this recipe:

  • 1lb of red meat
  • 1 spoonfull of cooking oil
  • 1 cup of water
  • 1 big round onion
  • 2 tomatoes
  • 4 eggs

that you've got your ingredients ready, lets get to cook!

  1. pre heat a pan and when its hot enough put oil and meat in there, then cooked to your prefered term
  2. dice the tomatoe and the onion, then add it to the same pan the meat is being cooked in until the onion is golden
  3. serve your meat with the onions and tomatoes on top
  4. fry your eggs and put it on top of the meat along the onions and tomatoes
  5. you're done!
