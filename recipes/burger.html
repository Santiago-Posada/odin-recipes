The Perfect Burger!

The good ol' classical burger recipe, you cant go wrong with this one!

The following list will give you the needed ingredients for this recipe:

Ingredients

  • 1 large egg
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 cup fine dry bread crumbs

that you've got your ingredients ready, lets get to cook!

Recipe

  1. Preheat your outdoor grill or pan, the results may differ on what you use, but it'll be equally delicious.
  2. Take a bowl (medium sized) and quickly whisk egg, salt, and pepper together.
  3. Add ground beef and bread crumbs to the bowl, mix it with your hands or fork until its well blended!.
  4. This one's easy, Form the patties! into any shape or size you want.
  5. cook it. thats it, just put it on the grill or pan with some oil and cook it to whatever term you prefer.
  6. Serve and enjoy your burger!
